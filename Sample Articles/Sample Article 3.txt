**Article Title:** Addressing "Service Unavailable" Error Messages  
**Last Updated:** [Date]  
**Applicable Products:** Cloud Platform Users  

---

### Common Symptoms  
- Intermittent 503 errors during peak hours  
- Regional outage notifications in status dashboard  
- Failed API calls with "resource unavailable"  

---

### Step-by-Step Resolution  
1. **Confirm Outage Scope**  
   - Check internal status page (portal/status)  
   - Review regional incident reports  

2. **Customer Communication**  
   - Share estimated resolution time (if available)  
   - Offer manual data export via CSR Toolbox > Emergency Export  

3. **Compensation Protocol**  
   - Apply service credit using SLA Calculator  
   - Send outage follow-up template #OUTAGE-FOLLOWUP  

---

### Prevention Tips for Customers  
- Enable multi-region failover settings  
- Monitor webhook alerts for system status  
- Maintain local cache of critical data
